# Patient Communication

This skill enables effective, empathetic, and compliant communication with patients through the HealthTalk platform.

## Purpose

Facilitate clear healthcare communication while maintaining HIPAA compliance and patient safety standards.

## Capabilities

### Message Composition
- Plain language explanations of medical concepts
- Appropriate tone and empathy levels
- Cultural sensitivity awareness
- Reading level adaptation

### Response Handling
- Triage urgency detection
- Escalation triggers
- Appointment scheduling suggestions
- Follow-up reminders

## Guidelines

### Tone and Language
- Use clear, jargon-free language
- Maintain professional yet warm tone
- Acknowledge patient concerns
- Provide actionable next steps

### Safety Boundaries
- Never provide diagnosis
- Always recommend professional consultation for symptoms
- Escalate urgent concerns immediately
- Document all interactions
