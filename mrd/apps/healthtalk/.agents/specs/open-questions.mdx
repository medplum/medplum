# Open Questions

Decisions pending before or during development of the HealthTalk Patient Questionnaire Module.

---

## Product Decisions

### 1. Patient Language Text Quality Control

Who reviews patient-language question text before publish? Is there a mandatory clinical review step, or is the authoring clinician responsible?

### 2. Multiple Questionnaires Per Appointment

If a clinician attaches PHQ-9 and GAD-7 to one appointment, are they sent as one message or two separate messages? Does the patient complete them sequentially or can they choose order?

### 3. Clinician Override of Auto-Send

The spec includes a "Suppress auto-send" checkbox. Confirm whether this is per-appointment or per-patient (e.g. patient in crisis should never receive automated messages).

### 4. Score Visibility to Patient

After completing a scored questionnaire, should the patient see their own score? Or is it clinician-only? This has clinical implications (especially for high suicide risk scores).

### 5. Answer Amendment

Can a patient change an answer after submission? If yes, how is the `QuestionnaireResponse` amended and how is the previous version preserved for audit?

---

## Technical Decisions

### 6. MedMij R4 Questionnaire IG

Monitor `informatiestandaarden.nictiz.nl` for the formal R4 questionnaire profile publication. Update profile URL on release; no structural changes expected.

### 7. zib2024 Breaking Changes

Confirm with Nictiz whether zib2024 introduces breaking changes to the Task or Questionnaire profiles vs zib2020.

### 8. LOINC API Fallback

If `fhir.loinc.org` is unreachable during a builder import attempt, what should the builder show? Suggest: cache the last successful fetch per instrument code in MedPlum with a `lastFetchedAt` timestamp.

---

## Permissions & Access

### 9. Builder Role Permissions

Which HealthTalk roles can create and publish questionnaires? Is there a separation between "draft" and "publish" permissions?

---

## Integration Timing

### 10. Coachi Integration Timing

When should completed `QuestionnaireResponse` resources be forwarded to the patient's Coachi PGO? Immediately on completion, or at end of care episode?
