# Open Questions

These questions must be resolved before or during development.

---

## 1. Patient Language Text Quality Control

**Question:** Who reviews patient-language question text before publish? Is there a mandatory clinical review step, or is the authoring clinician responsible?

**Impact:** Builder workflow, publish permissions

---

## 2. Multiple Questionnaires per Appointment

**Question:** If a clinician attaches PHQ-9 and GAD-7 to one appointment, are they sent as one message or two separate messages? Does the patient complete them sequentially or can they choose order?

**Impact:** Task creation logic, patient UX

---

## 3. Clinician Override of Auto-Send

**Question:** The spec includes a "Suppress auto-send" checkbox. Confirm whether this is per-appointment or per-patient (e.g. patient in crisis should never receive automated messages).

**Impact:** Appointment UI, patient record flag

---

## 4. Score Visibility to Patient

**Question:** After completing a scored questionnaire, should the patient see their own score? Or is it clinician-only? This has clinical implications (especially for high suicide risk scores).

**Impact:** Form completion page, clinical policy

---

## 5. Answer Amendment

**Question:** Can a patient change an answer after submission? If yes, how is the `QuestionnaireResponse` amended and how is the previous version preserved for audit?

**Impact:** FHIR versioning, audit trail

---

## 6. MedMij R4 Questionnaire IG

**Question:** Monitor `informatiestandaarden.nictiz.nl` for the formal R4 questionnaire profile publication. Update profile URL on release; no structural changes expected.

**Impact:** Profile URL in Task resources

---

## 7. zib2024 Breaking Changes

**Question:** Confirm with Nictiz whether zib2024 introduces breaking changes to the Task or Questionnaire profiles vs zib2020.

**Impact:** Future migration scope

---

## 8. Builder Role Permissions

**Question:** Which HealthTalk roles can create and publish questionnaires? Is there a separation between "draft" and "publish" permissions?

**Impact:** RBAC configuration

---

## 9. Coachi Integration Timing

**Question:** When should completed `QuestionnaireResponse` resources be forwarded to the patient's Coachi PGO? Immediately on completion, or at end of care episode?

**Impact:** PGO sync workflow

---

## 10. LOINC API Fallback

**Question:** If `fhir.loinc.org` is unreachable during a builder import attempt, what should the builder show?

**Suggestion:** Cache the last successful fetch per instrument code in MedPlum with a `lastFetchedAt` timestamp.

**Impact:** Builder error handling
