# Data Retention Policy

## Purpose

This compliance rule defines retention periods for different data categories
and ensures proper data lifecycle management.

## Retention Periods

| Data Category | Retention Period | Legal Basis |
|---------------|------------------|-------------|
| Medical records | 10 years after last treatment | Medical regulations |
| Audit logs | 7 years | Compliance requirements |
| Session data | 24 hours after logout | Security best practice |
| Consent records | Duration of relationship + 5 years | GDPR requirement |
| Communication logs | 3 years | Business requirement |

## Deletion Requirements

### Secure Deletion

- Use cryptographic erasure where possible
- Overwrite data with random bytes
- Remove from all backup systems
- Document deletion with audit trail

### Exceptions

Data may be retained beyond standard periods for:

1. Active legal proceedings
2. Regulatory investigations
3. Ongoing medical treatment
4. Explicit user request (with valid basis)

## Implementation

```typescript
interface RetentionPolicy {
  category: string;
  retentionDays: number;
  legalBasis: string;
  deletionMethod: 'soft' | 'hard' | 'cryptographic';
}
```

## Audit Requirements

- Monthly review of data approaching retention limits
- Quarterly compliance reports
- Annual policy review and update
