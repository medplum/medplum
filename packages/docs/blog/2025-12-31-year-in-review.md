An incredible year of growth, innovation, and community. From v5 release to expanding our developer ecosystem, 2025 marked several milestones for Medplum's open-source healthcare platform.

<!-- truncate -->

As we close out 2025, the Medplum team would love to thank our community and customers for joining us on this journey.

We wanted to highlight a few memorable moments and reflect on all that happened during the year. It was a lot of fun, and huge thank you to the team who pushed so hard to make all these things happen.

✅ Released **[Medplum v5](/blog/v5-release)**, our most significant platform modernization to date, featuring full FHIR terminology validation, GraphQL Patch support, and comprehensive dependency upgrades to Node.js 22/24, PostgreSQL 14/18, and React 19

✅ Launched our **new brand identity**, reflecting our evolution as a leading open-source healthcare infrastructure platform.  See the details [here](https://www.medplum.com/brand).

✅ Hosted our first annual developer conference **[PlumCon 2025](/blog/plumcon-2025-materials)** in San Francisco, bringing together healthcare innovators from around the world

✅ Delivered **many new features and integrations**, including enhanced AI capabilities with [MCP server support](/blog/unlocking-healthcare-ai-medplum-support-mcp), [custom FHIR operations](/blog/custom-fhir-operations), [IHE IRA radiology reporting certification](/blog/ihe-ira-radiology-reporting), and expanded integrations with health information exchange, medications, and revenue cycle.

✅ A special thank you to those who contributed this year - we now have **157 talented contributors** from around the world building the future of healthcare together

✅ Incredible ecosystem growth: **1,462 pull requests**, **55 releases**, **551 issues resolved**, **2.1K GitHub stars**, and **500K+ Docker downloads** powering healthcare applications globally

✅ Added many wonderful customers, and several have made videos of their implementations. See [case studies](https://www.medplum.com/case-studies) and [PlumCon recap](/blog/plumcon-2025-materials#demo-showcase) for videos.

✅ Organized **[several events](/blog/events-calendar)** throughout this year, including [FHIR + AI for Life Sciences](/blog/fhir-ai-for-life-sciences), [Scheduling Agents for Healthcare Operations](/blog/scheduling-agents-unity-ai), and [Visualizing Patient Flow](https://youtu.be/QvC093fAGGU), building our community of healthcare developers


Thank you, dear reader, for being part of our community. See you [on Discord](https://discord.gg/medplum).
