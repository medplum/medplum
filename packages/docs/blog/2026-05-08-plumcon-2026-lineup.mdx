# PlumCon 2026 Agenda

On September 3, 2026 Medplum is hosting our second annual _developer conference_ PlumCon in San Francisco. The conference is by invitation, but please [Register Here](https://luma.com/7xjt6cmh).

This year's agenda brings together healthcare builders working across open source infrastructure, AI-enabled operations, Stanford Biodesign, and large-scale healthcare IT.

This agenda is preliminary and subject to change.
