# Medplum Compare Campaign

- **Be Honest and Data-Driven:** Base your comparisons on concrete, verifiable facts. You can't just claim you're better; you need to show it with features, pricing, and service offerings. This is part of the "competitive analysis" process that informs the "competitive marketing"
- **Focus on Differentiation:** Instead of just listing features, focus on the "why" behind the differences. Use the comparisons to highlight your unique strengths and how they solve specific customer problems better than the competition
- **Address "Emotional Hurdles":** As a co-founder and CTO, you know the technical challenges. Your marketing should also address the emotional ones. A "Medplum vs. X" article can help customers overcome common concerns about switching, such as the time commitment, the risk to their credibility, or the complacency of staying with a "good enough" solution
- **Use a Clear Framework:** A structured format is more trustworthy and easier to digest. The classic approach is a side-by-side comparison of features, pricing, and key benefits. This helps the reader quickly see the differences and similarities
