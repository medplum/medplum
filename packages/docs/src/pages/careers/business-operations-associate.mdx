# Business Operations Associate

![Business Operations Associate](/img/careers/business-operations-associate.webp)

## About the Role

We are looking for a **Business Operations Associate** with high agency and a rigorous, analytical mindset to join our team. In this role, you will act as the "glue" across the organization, ensuring tight operational processes, driving cross-functional initiatives, and managing critical relationships with our partners and stakeholders.

You will wear many hats—from managing compliance audits and financial data analysis to crafting high-impact sales presentations and coordinating industry events. If you are methodical, detail-oriented, and thrive in an environment where accountability is key, this role is for you.

## What You Will Do

**Internal Operations & Communications**

- **Cross-Functional Orchestration:** Manage the rhythm of the business by organizing cross-functional meetings. You will create agendas, track down action items, provide executive summaries, and ensure high-bandwidth collaboration.
- **Knowledge Management:** Maintain and improve internal documentation, templates, and "Canon" materials (standardized written docs, slides, and code) that serve the entire organization.
- **Programs & Compliance:** Support the administration of critical compliance frameworks (SOC II, HITRUST) and financial programs (R&D Tax Credits).

**Partnerships & External Relations**

- **Partner Management:** Serve as the primary operational contact for key ecosystem partners (e.g., Labs, RCM, Medications, Fax). You will maintain these relationships to ensure shared program advancement and goodwill.
- **Events & Conferences:** Oversee the planning and preparation for industry events, including engaging speakers, managing logistics, and ensuring the team is prepped for success.

**Analysis & Sales Support**

- **Data & Planning:** Gather and prepare analytical datasets used for strategic planning, business reviews, and finance purposes.
- **Sales Enablement:** Support the commercial team by creating high-quality proposal slides and assisting with the contracting process to ensure deals are documented correctly and close on time.

## About You

- You have exceptional written and oral communication skills and can distill complex ideas into clear, compelling narratives.
- You have strong ability to create professional, persuasive slides and presentations for various audiences.
- You have proven experience managing expectations and relationships with diverse groups, including partners, customers, community members, and prospects.
- You take extreme ownership. You identify gaps and fill them without needing to be asked. You possess a strong sense of accountability.
- You are comfortable working with data and approaching problems with a logical, structured mindset.
- You are methodical and detail-oriented—you ensure that nothing slips through the cracks.

## What Success Looks Like

- **Operational Tightness:** Processes run smoothly with no feeling of "loose ends."
- **On-Target Delivery:** Contracting, deployments, and internal projects are consistently executed on time and on budget.
- **High-Value Collaboration:** Major cross-functional meetings are viewed as high-bandwidth and valuable uses of time by all team members.
- **Partner Growth:** Vendor and partner relationships are strengthened, leading to tangible business benefits.
- **Lasting Assets:** You consistently produce "Canon" materials—resources that become the standard for the organization to use repeatedly.
