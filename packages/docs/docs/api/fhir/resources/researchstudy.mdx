# ResearchStudy

A process where a researcher or organization plans and then executes a series of steps intended to increase the field of healthcare-related knowledge. This includes studies of safety, efficacy, comparative effectiveness and other information about medications, devices, therapies and other interventional and investigative techniques. A <Link to="/docs/api/fhir/resources/researchstudy">ResearchStudy</Link> involves the gathering of information about human or animal subjects.
