# RelatedPerson

Information about a person that is involved in the care for a patient, but who is not the target of healthcare, nor has a formal responsibility in the care process.

  <TabItem value="usage" label="Usage">
<div> <a name="scope"></a> <p> <Link to="/docs/api/fhir/resources/relatedperson">RelatedPersons</Link> typically have a personal or non-healthcare-specific professional relationship to the patient. A <Link to="/docs/api/fhir/resources/relatedperson">RelatedPerson</Link>  resource is primarily used for attribution of information,  since <Link to="/docs/api/fhir/resources/relatedperson">RelatedPersons</Link> are often a source of information about the patient.  For keeping information about people for contact purposes for a patient, use a <Link to="/docs/api/fhir/resources/patient">Patient</Link>'s Contact element.  Some individuals may serve as both a <Link to="/docs/api/fhir/resources/patient">Patient</Link>'s Contact and a Related <Link to="/docs/api/fhir/resources/person">Person</Link>. </p> <p> Example <Link to="/docs/api/fhir/resources/relatedperson">RelatedPersons</Link> are: </p> <ul> <li>A patient's wife or husband</li> <li>A patient's relatives or friends</li> <li>A neighbor bringing a patient to the hospital</li> <li>The owner or trainer of a horse</li> <li>A patient's attorney or guardian</li> <li>A Guide Dog</li> </ul> </div>
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="relationships" label="Relationships">
<div> <p> The primary distinction between a <Link to="/docs/api/fhir/resources/practitioner">Practitioner</Link> and a <Link to="/docs/api/fhir/resources/relatedperson">RelatedPerson</Link> is based on whether: </p> <ul> <li> The person/animal operates on behalf of the care delivery organization over multiple patients (Practitioner) or, </li> <li> Where the person/animal is not associated with the organization, and instead is allocated tasks specifically for the <Link to="/docs/api/fhir/resources/relatedperson">RelatedPerson</Link>'s <Link to="/docs/api/fhir/resources/patient">Patient</Link> (RelatedPerson). </li> </ul> <p> A standard extension <a href="https://www.hl7.org/fhir/extension-practitioner-animalspecies.html">animalSpecies</a> can be used to indicate the species of a service animal. </p> </div>
