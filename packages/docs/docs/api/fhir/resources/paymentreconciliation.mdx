    <div> <a name="scope"></a>  <p> The <a href="/docs/api/fhir/resources/paymentreconciliation">PaymentReconciliation</a> resource provides the bulk payment details  associated with a payment by the payor for receivable amounts, such as for goods and services rendered by a provider to patients  covered by insurance plans offered by that payor. These are the payment reconciliation details which may align to the individual payment amounts indicated on discrete <a href="/docs/api/fhir/resources/claimresponse">ClaimResponses</a> or <a href="/docs/api/fhir/resources/invoice">Invoices</a> for example. </p> <p> Bulk payments need to provide a means to associate the amounts paid against specific <a href="/docs/api/fhir/resources/claim">Claims</a>, <a href="/docs/api/fhir/resources/invoice">Invoices</a>,  and other financial exchanges and adjustments, to the bulk payment itself in order to reconcile  accounts receivable. </p> <p>    </p>     <p>The <a href="/docs/api/fhir/resources/paymentreconciliation">PaymentReconciliation</a> resource is a "event" resource from a FHIR workflow perspective - see <a href="https://www.hl7.org/fhir/workflow.html#event">Workflow Event.</a></p>  <h3 class="self-link-parent"> Additional Information <a href="https://www.hl7.org/fhir/paymentreconciliation.html#13.9.1.1" title="link to here" class="self-link"></a></h3> <p> Additional information regarding electronic payment notice content and usage may be found at: </p>   <ul> <li><a href="https://www.hl7.org/fhir/financial-module.html#resource-status">Financial Resource Status Lifecycle</a>: how .status is used in the financial resources.</li> </ul>  </div>
