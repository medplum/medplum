# HITRUST Certification

Medplum has achieved HITRUST e1 certification for the Medplum Platform residing at Amazon Web Services (AWS). The HITRUST e1 Certification demonstrates that strong controls are in place to protect sensitive data and manage risk effectively, validated through independent third-party assessment under the HITRUST Assurance Program.

<a href="https://hitrustalliance.net/">
  <img src="/img/compliance/hitrust-e1-badge.svg" alt="HITRUST e1 Certification badge" width="200" height="200" />
</a>

Medplum achieved certification in partnership with [Prescient Security](https://prescientsecurity.com/), leveraging the [myCSF](https://hitrustalliance.net/product-tool/mycsf/) platform and [Vanta](https://www.vanta.com/) for continuous compliance monitoring.

Compliance documentation is available on [Medplum's Trust Center on Vanta](https://app.vanta.com/medplum.com/trust/ybe9my9rkc6ok8yijm04h). Current and prospective customers can email [info@medplum.com](mailto:info@medplum.com) for more details, including access to the certification letter.

## Process Notes

For those pursuing HITRUST certification using Medplum as a primary application backend, we can support your certification process by providing documentation and evidence of our controls.
