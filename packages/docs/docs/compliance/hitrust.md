# HITRUST Certification

Medplum is pursuing HITRUST certification in partnership with [Prescient Security](https://www.yourprescient.com/), leveraging the [myCSF](https://hitrustalliance.net/product-tool/mycsf/) platform and [Vanta](https://www.vanta.com/) for continuous compliance monitoring. This certification will provide our customers with additional assurance of our commitment to security and regulatory compliance.

Current and prospective customers can email [info@medplum.com](mailto:info@medplum.com) for more details.

## Process Notes

For those pursuing HITRUST certification using Medplum as a primary application backend, we can support your certification process by providing documentation and evidence of our controls.
